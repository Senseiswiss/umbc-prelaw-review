Submission Guidelines

  • Open to all UMBC undergraduate students in any major
  • Co-authored submissions are welcome
  • Word count: 5,000–20,000 words (including footnotes)
  • Bluebook (21st ed.) citation format required
  • Double-spaced, Times New Roman 12pt, 1-inch margins
  • Include a 150–300 word abstract
  • Remove all identifying information from the manuscript (blind review)
  • Submit as a single PDF file (max 4 MB)

Review Process

  • All submissions undergo double-blind peer review
  • Decisions communicated within 6–8 weeks
  • Authors may be asked to revise and resubmit
  • Simultaneous submissions to other journals are discouraged

Questions?

  • Email us at umbcprelawreview@umbc.edu
  • Response within 2–3 business days
